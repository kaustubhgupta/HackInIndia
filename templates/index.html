About

Taskify is a reminder application that is not your regular application but an ML-based application. In this application, not only you but your friends, family, and colleagues can set alarms, reminders, and tasks on your phone. Just put in your reminder/task in the text field and our application will sort the task according to its urgency level.
For example,
1. I need to do this task today only.
2. I will do this tomorrow.
Our application will pick up the words (tomorrow,meeting, reminding) and will ask for a confirmation to set a reminder for the meeting.
The 1st task will be sorted on top and frequent reminders will be given on your email id and notification bar on an android device.
